ABOUT ME

I am very passionate about the art of capturing a photograph, and I like to express my worldview through a photograph, I am more into a food, product and travel photography. These areas in photography excite me and let me transform my creativity into a photograph. I have been doing photography for 4 years now, and the gears I am using are Nikon D7200 with Sigma 17-50 f/2.8, Nikon ed vr II 70-300mm f/4.5-5.6 and a 50mm f/1.8. My dream kit includes Nikon D850, Nikkon 70-200mm f/2.8, Sigma 18-35 f/1.8, Nikkon 200-500mm f/5.6.
